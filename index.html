Who Am I ?

Hi, I'm Petek. I'm a student/developer who have chemistry background.I'm studying for a master’s degree in information technologies at Ege University, İzmir. Also I have 3.71 GPA on Organic Chemistry Master Degree and I was only student who has been selected in interview for Chemistry Department in my term at Uludag University.

I soon realized that I wanted the projects I developed to be directly related to technology. I love to strive any kinds of problems and exchanging idea with different people. And of course, I want my works to touch lifes of people to make their life easier. Currently I'm improving my knowledge of frontend technologies such as JavaScript, HTML5 and CSS3. My main goal is gonna be a fullstack developer. At the same time I wanna improve my git skills like a gitman.

So I took some excellent online courses about fundamental computer science theory on edX and Udemy.I hold the Professional Certificate of Computer Science Essentials for Software Development from PennX (Online Education System of University of Pennsylvania) among the other certificates from Udemy about software development. And I participated "Women Software Crafters Empowerment Program" at Microsoft Office in Istanbul. In the program, some important topics such as clean code, TDD, code kata, pair programming, agile was mentioned.

At odd moments, I write a blog about software development. That is my LinkedIn page, GitHub repository and Medium Blog for you to check out:
